Life History

Most recently I have been a Teaching Assistant at Boise Code Works after graduating from the full-stack development class there. During that time I learned a variety of languages and platforms including: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Angular. We were also introduced to Node, .NET and C#.
My “capstone project” for this class was the conception, development and design of a product I named “Musical-Keymaster.” My team of two other students and myself produced this program in just over a month. The app takes in a pasted chord sheet and transposes the chord references to whatever key the end user desires. There is also a private storage area for users to keep their stash of songs.

Prior to that, I worked for the Divine Lotus Tea Company (aka The Shangri La Tea Room & Restaurant) based in Boise.
While there I learned to manage my time efficiently. I also used my skills in how to be productive in a loosely structured, low supervision environment.
In order to use my time there most efficiently, I developed and organized the herb and tea inventory which optimized my (and other employees) ability to find stocked items. In conjunction with this, I created an inventory checklist which was used for the reorder process when items periodically went out of stock. I also built a spreadsheet to track gross income from retail tea sales and graph them quarterly.
